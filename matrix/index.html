Introduction

KNOW MORE ABOUT METRIX PAKISTAN

Metrix Pakistan is a pioneering organization dedicated to fostering technological education and entrepreneurship in the rural areas of Pakistan. Founded by Hassan Nisar in May 2022, its mission is to create a thriving ecosystem in underprivileged regions of Pakistan. Through innovative initiatives, Metrix Pakistan aims to empower local communities by providing access to technology and educational resources, thereby catalyzing economic development and social progress.

  • ✔Organized Khyber Pakhtunkhwa's largest tech summit in October 2022, fostering innovation and collaboration.
  • ✔Conducted free medical camps to provide healthcare to underserved communities.
  • ✔Hosted a conference on cyberspace safety with the Pakistan Telecommunication Authority to promote digital literacy and cybersecurity awareness.

Meet Our Founder & CEO

At Metrix Pakistan, we believe in the transformative power of technology. Our goal is to empower the youth of KP by providing them with the tools to excel in this digital age. Through initiatives like the Metrix Tech Summit, Tech Fest, and Pakistan Medical Camps, we’re creating spaces for innovation, learning, and growth.Together, let’s build a future where technology bridges gaps, fuels creativity, and drives progress.

As the founder & CEO of Metrix Pakistan, I invite you to join us on this remarkable journey. Together, let’s chart a path towards a future where technology is a tool for empowerment, education is a universal right, and innovation is a way of life. Our success story is shaped by the communities we uplift, the young minds we nurture, and the partnerships we forge
